About this Piece

The ideal three-seat sofa featuring one seat with a chaise lounge addition, one seat with an ottoman, and one seat for the

chiropractor who loves to sit upright with their feet on the floor. Like everything we make, our modular design is incredibly easy

to move and packed with all the clever, life-changing features you expect from Burrow, starting with a built-in USB charger.

Customizable

Create your ideal sectional sofa with five fabric colors, six leg
